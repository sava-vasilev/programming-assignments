BudgetGo

This app was an assignment for a subject during the first semester in Fontys. It as a group project for Experience Design(EDO) that was assignet to me and a couple of friends.

The group participants are: Me, Iulia Toderascu, Francisco Marco, Daniil Parokonnyy, Bora Özertürk, Artjoms Figurins and Lihn Tran.

Currently there isn't a working version of the app but only a prototype and a wirechart. It's purpose is to propose places to eat for a certain budget and size of friend group. After creating an account the user can look up places with a set of prefferences and can also look up information about the place, save as bookmark or book a ticket or a table.
